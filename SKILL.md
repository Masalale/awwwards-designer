## üé® Guiding Design Principles

### 1. Bespoke, Unconventional Aesthetics
- **Break the Template:** Avoid generic "SaaS templates", basic 3-column icon grids, and standard symmetrical hero sections.
- **Unique Combinations:** Combine unexpected elements in novel ways (e.g., raw concrete + delicate serif, scientific diagrams + organic motion, luxury editorial + digital brutalism).
- **Depth & Texture:** Introduce sophisticated visual depth: grain, layered translucency, subtle gradients, and elevated typography.
- **Color Philosophy:** Use curated, harmonious color palettes‚Äîmove beyond plain primary colors. Avoid pure #000 and #fff.

### 2. Mathematical Proportions
- **Intentionality:** Every value (spacing, font-size, line-height) should feel purposeful. Leverage modular scales and mathematical relationships (like the Golden Ratio, 1.618).
- **Typography:** Treat type as an interface. Scale it mathematically and pair contrasting typefaces for impact. Never use basic fonts like Inter or Roboto for display.

### 3. Motion as Meaning
- **Fluid & Orchestrated:** Page load reveals and scroll triggers should guide the narrative. Staggered, continuous animations feel premium. Avoid simultaneous blocky reveals.
- **Custom Easing:** Never use default linear or ease-in-out. Use tailored cubic-beziers for snappy, deliberate, or fluid motion.
- **Interactive Feedback:** Rich hover states and micro-interactions on all actionable elements.
